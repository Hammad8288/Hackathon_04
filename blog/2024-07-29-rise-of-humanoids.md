Humanoid robots, once a concept of science fiction, are rapidly becoming a reality. Companies like Boston Dynamics and Tesla are pushing the boundaries of what's possible, creating machines that can walk, run, and even perform complex tasks.

This post is the first in a series where we will explore the exciting world of humanoid robotics. We'll delve into the hardware, the software, and the ethical implications of this transformative technology.
