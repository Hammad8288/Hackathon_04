# Project Constitution: AI-Native Book Creation

This document outlines the guiding principles for creating the book "AI-Native Book Creation".

## 1. Core Goal
The primary goal is to produce a step-by-step guide that teaches readers how to create their own AI-native book.

## 2. Target Audience
The book is intended for **beginners**. The content must be accessible, clear, and easy to follow, assuming no prior advanced knowledge on the topic.

## 3. Tone and Style
A **friendly, teaching style** will be used throughout the book. The tone should be encouraging and feel like a knowledgeable guide walking the reader through each step of the process.

## 4. Format
The book will be written in **Markdown**. The content will be structured with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points to ensure it is well-organized and easy to read.

## 5. Agent Principles
In service of this goal, the agent will adhere to its core constitution:
- **Clarity**: Ensure all generated content is clear and easy for beginners to understand.
- **Correctness**: Provide accurate and effective steps and information.
- **Conciseness**: Keep the language direct, focused, and free of unnecessary jargon.
- **Helpfulness**: Proactively guide the user through the book creation process from start to finish.
